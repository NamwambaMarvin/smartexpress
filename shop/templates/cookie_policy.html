Mzuri Express Appliances Uganda Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how Mzuri Express Appliances Uganda (referred to as "we", "us", or "our" in this policy) uses them on our website, www.mzuriexpress.com (the "Website").

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit(s).

What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

We use several different types of cookies on the Website. These can be categorized as follows:

Essential Cookies:

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our Website. They enable you to navigate the Website and use its essential features, such as accessing secure areas or using shopping cart services. These cookies do not collect any information that could be used for marketing or advertising purposes. Disabling these cookies may impair your experience on the Website.

Functionality Cookies:

These cookies allow the Website to remember your preferences (such as language or region) and provide you with more personalized features. For example, they may be used to remember your login information so you don't have to enter it every time you visit the Website. Disabling these cookies may mean that some features of the Website will not function as intended.

Analytics Cookies:

These cookies collect information about how you use the Website, such as the pages you visit and how long you spend on them. This information is used to improve the Website and user experience. We may use third-party analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to collect and analyze this information. These tools typically use their own cookies to track user interactions. You can learn more about Google Analytics and its use of cookies here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies?hl=en-US . You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Advertising Cookies:

These cookies may be placed on your device by us or by third-party advertisers. They are used to deliver targeted advertising to you on our Website and other websites you visit. These cookies track your browsing activity across different websites and build a profile of your interests. This information is then used to show you advertisements that are likely to be relevant and interesting to you.
