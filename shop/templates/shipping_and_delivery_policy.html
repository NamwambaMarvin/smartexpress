Shipping and Delivery Policy

How does Mzuri Express deliver?

For deliveries around Kampala, Wakiso and Entebbe districts, we use our local delivery agents. Our delivery means include a motorcycle, tricycle, or a truck. For deliveries outside the mentioned areas, we process orders through a delivery courier that transports your item to your location.

How much is the delivery?

Delivery fees for goods between 0kgs and 50kg is 10% of the price of the product

Delivery fees for goods above 50kgs is 25% of the price of the product

NOTE: We share only your contact information specifically your name and phone number with the party to deliver your item. Therefore, you should expect a delivery personnel or a courier service to contact you as soon as your order is ready for delivery.

All prices of the products on this website do not include delivery fees

How long does my order take to arrive?

Areas within Kampala, Wakiso and Entebbe. It takes a maximum of 3 hours to get your ordered item delivered to you.

At our physical location (Aponye Mall Opposite Old Taxi Park, Shop number MA22). Buying an item takes less than 5 minutes.

Does Mzuri Express ship internationally?

No, we do not ship internationally. We deliver only within Uganda. You can order an item from anywhere in the world but the delivery location should be within Uganda.

Are there delivery restrictions?

No, there are no delivery restrictions currently.

How can I track my order?

Get in touch with us. We assign a sales personnel to each and every client that orders. When you want to track your item, you can call them directly or send a WhatsApp message.

Do you offer free shipping? If so, what are the conditions?

We don't offer free shipping.

What are your order processing times?

Order processing time is about 30 minutes on working days from 9am to 6pm. On weekends, it is 2 hours.

Do you ship to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses?

No, we do not ship to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Yes you can only if you inform us through a phone call or messaging.
